My Story

I was born and raised in Kingston, Jamaica. I was born on October 16, 2000 at 6 a.m. I have no idea why I decided to pop out so early in the morning. I despise early mornings. I'm a night owl through and through. Don't get me wrong, I admire anyone who is a morning person. It seems so peaceful and calming to wake up at dawn and have that time to yourself. Trust me, I'm jealous. I wish I could gracefully wake up at 5 a.m., make my tea, meditate, go for a morning run and make myself a delicious breakfast - all before 8 a.m. But as soon as my alarm starts blaring, my brain instantly fights attacks me for disturbing its rest. Trust me, it is not pretty.
